Stereoscope Converter

Convert a single existing photo into a stereo pair, a red/cyan anaglyph and a glasses-free wiggle — fully in your browser, no account required.

Two engines ship together: Local (on-device depth + parallax, WebGPU/WASM) and AI (OpenRouter, your own credits via OAuth). Apple Live Photo / MV-HEVC spatial export is not possible in-browser and is out of scope; Google Motion Photo export arrives later.
